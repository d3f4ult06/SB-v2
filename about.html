About Starbucks Philippines

Starbucks Philippines is part of the global Starbucks Coffee Company, dedicated to inspiring and nurturing the human spirit – one person, one cup, and one neighborhood at a time.

Since opening its first store in the Philippines, Starbucks has been committed to providing high-quality coffee and exceptional customer experiences. We strive to create a warm and welcoming environment for all our customers.

Sustainability

We believe in making a positive impact on the communities we serve and the environment through sustainable practices.

Quality Coffee

Our mission is to inspire and nurture the human spirit through our coffee and community involvement.

Community

We create a space where everyone feels welcome and valued, fostering connections every day.

Thank you for being part of the Starbucks Philippines journey. We look forward to serving you your favorite coffee and more.
